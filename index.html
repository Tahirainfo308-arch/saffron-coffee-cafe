Our Story

More Than Just a Cup of Coffee

It began in 2015 — a small roaster, a second-hand espresso machine, and an unwavering obsession with the perfect extraction. Today, Saffron has become a beloved Lahore institution, but our philosophy is unchanged: every origin tells a story, and every cup you hold should be worth your morning.

We travel the world sourcing direct-trade beans — partnering directly with smallholder farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Indonesia — then roast in small weekly batches to lock in peak freshness.

  • ✓ Ethically sourced, direct-trade beans
  • ✓ Roasted weekly in small batches
  • ✓ Vegan & gluten-free options available
  • ✓ Award-winning specialty baristas

Why Saffron

The Difference You Can Taste

We believe great coffee doesn't happen by accident. It's the result of obsessive attention at every step.

🌍

Direct-Trade Sourcing

We visit our farm partners personally. Cutting out middlemen means better pay for farmers and better beans for you.

🔥

Weekly Small-Batch Roasting

Roasted in batches of 5 kg every Monday, so what's in your cup is never more than 7 days from the roaster.

🎓

SCA-Certified Baristas

Every Saffron barista holds a Specialty Coffee Association certification and trains monthly to sharpen their craft.

🌱

Sustainably Operated

Compostable packaging, zero food waste policy, and carbon-offset delivery make every visit guilt-free.

❤️

Community First

We host free cupping events, a monthly open-mic, and partner with local artists to make Saffron a neighbourhood hub.

✨

Inclusive Menu

Oat, almond, and coconut milk always available. Vegan and gluten-free options clearly labelled across the full menu.
